BEST

Mumbai city bus route no 234 is operated by BEST. In Mumbai there are seven bus lines: BEST, KDMT, KMT, MBMT, NMMT, TMT, and, VVMT. BEST is the company that primarily operates the public bus transport service in Mumbai and runs multiple number of city buses. BEST runs many buses between Vesave / Yari Road Bus Station and Jogeshwari Bus Station on daily basis.

Metro and local

The Mumbai Metro is a rapid transit (MRT) system serving the city of Mumbai and the wider Mumbai Metropolitan Region in Maharashtra, India. Mumbai Metropolitan Region Development Authority is the responsible authority for maintaining the metro system.Mumbai Suburban Railway is a 390 kilometer (240 mi) suburban railway network that began its operations in 1853.0 It consists of exclusive inner suburban railway lines augmented by commuter rail on outlying suburbs to serve the Mumbai Metropolitan Region and carries more than 7.5 million commuters daily.1 Popularly known as locals, the three lines are the Central Main Line, the Western Line and the Harbour Line which run round-the-clock

Airport

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (IATA: BOM, ICAO: VABB) is an international airport serving Mumbai and the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR). It is the second busiest airport in the country in terms of total and international passenger traffic after Delhi, and was the 14th busiest airport in Asia and 41st busiest airport in the world by passenger traffic in calendar year 2019.
